# 1.3 Understand competitive landscape
Goal: Compare Focus Bear with Other Productivity Apps

## **_What makes Focus Bear different from these Apps?_**
Below are what I think to be the advantages of the other applications:

- Freedom: mainly blocks websites and applications, but has proper cross-device blocking, internet blocking
- Cold Turkey: main feature is its website block scheduling 
- Tiimo: visually creative widgets, personalization, and has mood tracking
- Routinery: building routine consistency

But Focus Bear's advantage combines and somewhat improves upon the advantages of the previous four applications. iIt has combined features of habit stacking and blocking, utilizies active blocking through pomodoro breaks, all-device aggressive application and website blocking/

## **_If you were a user, why would you choose Focus Bear over competitors?_**
Focus bear is flexible, and understandably so as my wake-up and sleep times vary everyday. 

## **_What’s one feature that other apps have that Focus Bear doesn’t?_**
A visual timeline of activities, where we have the mourning routine and evening routine separated as a block in the timeline. This allows me to visualize what can I do with my day.

## **_Based on your research, what’s one improvement you think Focus Bear could make?_**
"Flow-state" detection in Focus bear, that allows the user to dictate extension of their focus time in a certain task.
