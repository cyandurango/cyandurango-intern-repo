# 2.1 Set Up AI Tools for Development
Goal: Enhance your coding workflow by exploring AI-powered tools that assist with development.

## **_Which AI tools did you try?_**
I have tried all three AI tools listed here (Github Copilot, ChatGPT & Claude), and I have mainly used Gemini.

## **_What worked well? What didn’t?_**
I tasked each of the AI's that I have used to do the following:
- generate a code of a _factorial function with memoization_; 

- debug an _errored bubble sort algorithm_ code; and

- enhance my understanding on _binary search trees and AVL_

All AI tools generate somewhat the same outputs in these tasks. What worked was how logical and simple they explained each step, but this depends on the user's existing knowledge. Copilot is more logical, Claude provides an intuitive analogy, and ChatGPT is simple and good for beginner coders. 

I prefer AI tools to be specific in the context of what they prompted, to which, in my opinion this is where Copilot and Gemini stands out. 

## **_When do you think AI is most useful for coding?_**
To me, AI is most useful when I am already stuck in a problem for hours and can't solve it with my current understanding and expertise. I'll have AI generate the code, then I'll debug and tweak some adjustments before having AI re-check my adjustments.
