## 1.5 - Understanding of Focus Bear's mission and vision

Goal: Explore the Focus Bear website to learn about the company’s mission, vision, and key features.

### **_Why do you think Focus Bear was created?_**

Focus Bear was created and built with research-backed data to help in making the application work well for neurodivergent people to help them focus. And in Focus Bear's website, they listed five features that they've created for such a task:

1. Cross-device distraction blockers to remove technological distractions
2. Focus-boosting morning routine activities to help users start the day right.
3. Morning and evening routine habit trackers to facilitate decision-making
4. The use of visual cues that increase focus and enhance concentration.
5. Goal tracking statistics and metrics to increase motivation.

### **_What problem is Focus Bear solving?_**

The main problem is ADHD's negative impacts to productivity, learning, relationships, finance, and to almost every aspect of life. These negative impacts are brought upon by the following performance challenges, typically experienced by neurodivergent people:

1. Distractions due to technological use
2. Detachment from hyper-focused states.
3. Late-night usage of technology leading to loss of focus on the next day
4. the lack of meta-cognitive strategies that eventually lead to a loss of planning and scheduling.
5. Loss of regularity in routines.

### **_Why do you think this mission is important?_**

This mission is important as it helps neurodivergent people to be more productive to all aspects of their lives, through blocking distractions and guiding them through their daily routines. It allows them to learn how to live with their disorder, while still making significant impacts on anywhere they go: in school, at work, or simply in their homes.

I may have reasons to believe that I may be in the neurodivergent spectrum, but I have not gotten myself diagnosed yet. One of those reasons is the fact that all five performance challenges that I listed have applied to me.
