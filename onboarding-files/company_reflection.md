## **_Why do you think Focus Bear was created?_**
Focus Bear was created and built with research-backed data to help in making the application work well for neurodivergent people to help them focus, 

## **_What problem is Focus Bear solving?_**
The main problem is ADHD's negative impacts to productivity, learning, relationships, finance, and to almost every aspect of life.

## **_Why do you think this mission is important?_**
This mission is important as it helps neurodivergent people to be more productive to all aspects of their lives, through blocking distractions and guiding them through their daily routines.

Focus Bear's work in helping other neurodivergent people aligns with my main interest of utilizing my knowledge and skills to help the greater good.
